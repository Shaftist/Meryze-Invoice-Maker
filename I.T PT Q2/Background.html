"Your Donation, Their Future"

Our Project

Our project "Your Donation, Their Future" aims to gather donations for the partner institution Madrasah.
The donations would be used in order to get new school equipment
such as whiteboards, books, and other school equipment
for the Madrasah Education Institution.

This would be achieved through online posters which would be posted
in different social media platforms in order to increase chances of people seeing it.




Significance of the project

Our project "Your Donation, Their Future" is aimed to make Madrasah more known and to help Madrasah get new school equipment/materials. "Madrasah" our partner institution is a Muslim education institution it helps Muslim children learn the fundamentals of their religious obligations, social responsibilities and privillages. Our project will help through making Madrasah more known as having more popularity would help in gaining donations and possible sponsors. Our projects main objective is to help Madrasah gain new school equipment/materials to help the students in learning their religion.

Rationale

The project could help by giving more school equipment
to the Institution and help the students by giving them
better quality equipment in order to learn more through
the books and giving the teachers better equipment
in order to explain better to the students.

Project Objectives

The main goal of the project is to promote the
Madrasah institution in order to give equitable
education to Muslim students. To achieve this,
we will post e-posters in different online platforms
to promote Madrasah, hopefully this will be able
to increase the institution’s funds.
