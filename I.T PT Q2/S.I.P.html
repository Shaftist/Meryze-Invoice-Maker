Science Investigatory Project

Amount of Vegetable/Fruit waste effect on tomato plant growth

Title



Will vegetable peelings be an efficient compost to effectively deliver the necessary nutrients to our tomato plant? And make it grow faster.

Problem



IF vegetable peelings are used as the compost to our tomato plant, THEN our tomato plant will grow faster.

Hypothesis





Our project "Amount of vegetable/fruit waste Compost effect on tomato plant growth" aims to find at which amount of vegetable/fruit waste compost will be able to help the plant grow faster. Our project aims to help farmers or people looking to plant their own vegetables in finding out the right amount of compost in order to maximize and quicken the procces of planting a tomato plant whilst keeping the plant healthy and alive. We plan to record 5 set-ups containing different amounts of compost and record their growth every week and calculate their growth rate every week.

Significance of the study
